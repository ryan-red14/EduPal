# EduPal

EduPal is an AI-first learning platform built for Kenyan students and designed around the Kenyan curriculum. It brings together AI-powered learning, study resources, and productivity tools to create a personalized and engaging learning experience.

At the heart of EduPal is **Orbis**, EduPal's intelligent AI learning assistant. Orbis is designed to explain concepts, answer curriculum-based questions, guide students through difficult topics, and provide personalized learning support tailored to each student's progress and learning goals.

## Current MVP

The EduPal MVP includes the foundation of the platform with features such as:

* Orbis AI Learning Assistant
* Academic Analytics
* Personalized Learning
* Digital Library
* Smart Notebook
* Flashcards
* Calendar & Planner
* Group Projects
* Gamification & Leaderboards
* Career Guidance
* Student Portfolio
* Learning Tools & Resources

Some features are still under active development and may not yet be fully functional. They are included as part of the MVP to demonstrate EduPal's direction and long-term vision.

## Future Plans

EduPal is continuously evolving. Planned features include:

* Advanced AI-generated quizzes, exams, and personalized revision plans
* AI marking with detailed feedback and explanations
* Interactive virtual science labs and simulations
* Teacher dashboard and school management system
* Parent portal for monitoring student progress
* Real-time collaboration and study spaces
* AI-powered performance predictions and learning recommendations
* Offline learning with seamless cloud synchronization
* Interactive multimedia lessons and educational content
* National academic competitions and community challenges
* Integration with school systems and educational platforms
* Support for additional curricula beyond the Kenyan curriculum
